# Plans & Pricing (Guidance)

> **Pricing note**: This is a recommended packaging model. Final pricing depends on connector scope, volume, and support/SLA.

## 1) Growth
**Target**: mid-market advertisers / in-house teams

- Tenants: up to 3
- Key rotation: ALERT mode
- Policy DSL: baseline templates (Google/Amazon)
- Audit exports: monthly
- Support: business hours

**Price**: $3,000–$6,000 / month

## 2) Enterprise
**Target**: large brands / agencies managing many accounts

- Tenants: up to 25 (expandable)
- Key rotation: ENFORCE available (recommended)
- Policy DSL: custom constraints + approvals
- Audit exports: weekly + on-demand
- SLA: 99.9%
- Support: 24x5

**Price**: $12,000–$30,000 / month

## 3) Governance+
**Target**: regulated orgs / public companies / global operations

- Tenants: unlimited (contracted)
- Key rotation: ENFORCE mandatory
- 2-person approval for policy changes (SoD)
- Evidence Pack automation: per-change + monthly + quarterly
- Dedicated TAM + incident response
- SLA: 99.95% (optional)

**Price**: $40,000+ / month (custom)

## Add-ons
- Extra connectors (per provider)
- Dedicated VPC / private deployment
- Advanced alerting (PagerDuty/Slack/etc.)
- Quarterly controls review & auditor workshop
