# SLA (Proposed)

## Availability
- Growth: Best effort
- Enterprise: 99.9% monthly uptime
- Governance+: 99.95% optional

## Support Response
- P1 (system down / ENFORCE outage): 30 min (Enterprise), 15 min (Governance+)
- P2 (degraded / connector partial failure): 4 hours
- P3 (general): 1 business day

## Change Management
- Policy change propagation: < 5 minutes
- Approval → execution queueing: < 2 minutes typical

## Data Retention
- Audit logs: 365 days default (configurable)
- Incident timeline: 365 days
- Evidence packs: 7 years recommended for regulated orgs
