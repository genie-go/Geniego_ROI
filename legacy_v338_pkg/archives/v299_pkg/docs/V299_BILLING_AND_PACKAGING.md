# Billing & Packaging (V299)

## Plans
A plan defines:
- base monthly price
- included events/contacts/connectors
- overage pricing

## Usage metering
usage_counters_daily collects daily counts.
Invoice preview uses the plan + usage.

## Tenant policy templates
You can bundle plans with policy templates to create packages like:
- Growth Starter (light approvals)
- Enterprise Secure (strict scopes + approvals + audit)
