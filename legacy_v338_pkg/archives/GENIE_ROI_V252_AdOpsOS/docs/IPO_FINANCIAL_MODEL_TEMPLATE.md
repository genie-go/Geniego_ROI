# IPO Financial Model Template (V250)

## Key drivers
- # of ad accounts managed
- Avg spend per account
- Take rate (subscription + performance)
- Gross margin (compute + support)
- CAC / Payback
- NRR (net revenue retention)

## Example (placeholder)
Year 1:
- 200 accounts, $50k/mo spend, take rate 1.5%
- ARR ~ $1.8M
Year 3:
- 2,000 accounts -> ARR ~ $18M
Year 5:
- 10,000 accounts -> ARR ~ $90M

## Metrics to track
- NRR > 120%
- Gross margin > 75%
- CAC payback < 9 months
