## 결정 (핵심)

1. **★실측 정직·Ledger/Payout 엔진 부재·핵심 패턴 실 정본 존재**: **고객 Cashback Ledger/Balance/Wallet/Withdrawal/Payout 엔진은 부재(NOT_APPLICABLE·grep 0)**. 그러나 핵심 패턴의 **실 정본이 존재**: **CouponRedeem**(원자적 조건부 UPDATE=**Double Redemption Detection 정본**·구독 쿠폰·KEEP_SEPARATE)·**point_discount**(마켓 포인트 체크아웃 사용=Order Credit 실 사례·읽기수집)·**Idempotency**(UNIQUE)·**fxToKrw**(FX)·Point Ledger 4-3(Append-only·Balance=Ledger reconciliation 상태 계약). **★핵심 정직: Payout은 outbound(테넌트→고객 지급)인데 현행 pg_settlement은 inbound(테넌트 수취)로 정반대 방향·Payout/Withdrawal/Recipient Verification(KYC)=완전 신설**. **기존 CouponRedeem 원자성/Idempotency/fxToKrw·Point Ledger 4-3 패턴 재사용(중복 엔진 금지)**. 지어내기·NO_DATA/오탐 금지·본 Ledger=멀티테넌트 고객용 미래 cashback 전방호환 계약.

2. **기존 Redemption 원자성/Idempotency/FX 재사용·중복 금지(§58)**: **Redemption 원자성=CouponRedeem 정본·Idempotency=UNIQUE·FX=fxToKrw 단일·Order Credit=point_discount**. Provider별 독립 Cashback Ledger Model 금지. inbound 정산(pg)↔outbound Payout 오혼입 금지.

3. **Accrual≠Entry·Ledger≠Balance·Append-only·Hash Chain(§4.1·§4.2·§4.6·§9~§12)**: Available 검증 Accrual만 Ledger Credit Entry. Ledger(이력)≠Balance(시점 계산·재계산 가능). 모든 Entry Append-only(Sequence·Previous/Entry Hash·Effective Time·Source Event·Idempotency Key·Balance After). 기존 Entry 수정/삭제 금지→Reversal/Correction/Adjustment/Clawback Entry. **현행 hash chain 부재=신설**·Point Ledger 4-3 Append-only 정합.

4. **Balance 상태 분리·Source≠신뢰(§4.3·§4.7·§15·§43)**: Pending/Available/Reserved/Redeemed/Wallet Converted/Payable/Settled/Paid/Expired/Reversed/Clawback/Liability Balance 분리. **Source Balance 단일 신뢰 금지→Ledger-calculated Balance 비교**(Balance 계산 Contract·Point Ledger 4-3 정합).

5. **Redemption·Reservation·Conversion·Double-use 차단(§4.5·§4.10·§18~§28·§41)**: Reservation(Request/Reserve/Commit/Release/Expire)·Full/Partial Redemption·Allocation(여러 Accrual·FIFO/earliest-expiry)·Order/Invoice/Subscription Credit. Wallet/Store Conversion 양방향 연결(원 Cashback≠덮어쓰기)·**전환 후 원 Cashback 재사용 차단**. 동일 Transaction 이중 사용/지급 금지(Redemption ID·Payout ID·Idempotency Key). **★현행 정본**: CouponRedeem 원자적 조건부 UPDATE(이중 사용 방지)·point_discount(Order Credit).

6. **Redemption≠Payout·요청≠지급·Recipient Verification(§4.4·§4.8·§4.9·§29~§39)**: Redemption(주문/서비스 사용)≠Payout(외부 지급수단). 출금 요청(Requested/Verified/Approved/Settled/Processing/Paid/Failed/Returned/Reversed 분리)≠지급 완료. **Payout Recipient≠Beneficiary(Recipient Verification·Destination Ownership 확인)·금융계좌 원문 저장 금지(Masked)**. Settlement(Gross/Fee/Withholding/Net)≠Payout. Failed/Returned Payout 후 Balance Restoration·Fee Refund·Tax Adjustment. **현행**: Payout(outbound)/Withdrawal/Recipient Verification(KYC)=완전 부재(Part 4-5-1-3)·신설·pg_settlement=inbound 정반대.

7. **Ledger Integrity·Duplicate/Double 방지(§40·§41·§42)**: Sequence Gap·Hash Mismatch·Modified/Deleted Entry·Orphan·Over-redemption·Over-payout·Negative Balance·Cross-account/currency Integrity. Duplicate Payout(Provider Payout ID·Idempotency·Beneficiary+Amount+Time Window)·Double Redemption(동일 Reservation/Order/Entry/Accrual·Wallet 전환 후 재사용). **Duplicate 삭제 금지→Group/Winner/Excluded 보존**. **현행 정본**: CouponRedeem 원자성·UNIQUE.

8. **정직·무후퇴·Reconciliation/Coverage/Gap**: Cashback Ledger/Payout 엔진 부재=PROVIDER_LIMITATION/NOT_APPLICABLE(NO_DATA/오탐 금지). Source↔Ledger↔Provider↔Portal Balance·Redemption↔Order Credit·Wallet Conversion↔Destination·Withdrawal↔Settlement↔Payout·Failed/Returned↔Restoration Reconciliation. Critical Gap(Entry 수정/삭제·Balance 불일치·이중 사용·이중 Payout·Wallet 전환 후 재사용·Available 초과·Unverified Recipient Payout·Failed/Returned 후 미복구·Settlement↔Provider 불일치·Sequence/Hash 실패·Negative Balance·Provider Drift) 시 Access Review 차단.
