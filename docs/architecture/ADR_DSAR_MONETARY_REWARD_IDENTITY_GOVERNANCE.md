## 결정 (핵심)

1. **★실측 정직·Identity Governance 부재·재사용 기반 명시**: **현금성 보상의 Participant/Beneficiary/Payout Recipient/Beneficial Owner/Tax Subject를 구분하는 Identity Governance는 부재** — Identity Profile/Participant·Beneficiary Identity/Role Assignment/Payout Recipient·Beneficial Owner·Tax Subject Reference/Recipient Verification(KYC/KYB)/Shared Account/Multi-beneficiary Allocation/Identity Confidence/structured Alias·Merge·Unmerge/Canonical Organization/Affiliate·Creator·Seller monetary identity 전부 NOT_APPLICABLE(grep 0). 실체=**Customer person consolidation**(`crm_customers.identity_id`·재사용 기반)·**Referrer/Referee 역할분리**(referral_signup)·**운영 서브계정/위임**(PartnerPortal 공급망·AgencyPortal 대행). **★핵심 구분: crm identity=Customer 통합(보상 Participant/Beneficiary 아님·§57 Canonical Person Graph 재사용 대상·재생성 금지) · PartnerPortal/AgencyPortal=운영 계정/위임(보상 수령자 아님·KEEP_SEPARATE)**. 지어내기·NO_DATA/오탐 금지·본 Identity Model=멀티테넌트 고객용 미래 현금성 보상 Identity의 전방호환 계약.

2. **기존 Canonical Person/Organization/Partner/Seller 재사용·중복 금지(§57·§48)**: **Canonical Person=crm identity 단일**(재사용·중복 생성 금지)·Partner=PartnerPortal·Agency=AgencyPortal(도메인 분리). Reward 유형별 독립 Identity Registry 금지. Canonical Organization identity=부재→신설(중복 금지).

3. **Participant≠Beneficiary≠Payout Recipient≠Tax Subject≠Beneficial Owner(§5.1·5.2·§9·§21~24)**: Participant(발생 참여자)≠Beneficiary(경제적 이익)≠Payout Recipient(대표 계정)≠Tax Subject(법인)≠Beneficial Owner(별도 개인). 한 Reward에 다중 Role 동시(Triggering Customer/Reward Earner/Beneficiary/Payout Recipient). **§5.6 Payment Method Holder≠Beneficiary·§5.7 Account Owner≠Authorized User**. **현행 실 사례=Referral referrer(수령)≠referee(발생) 분리**·범용 Participant/Beneficiary/Role Assignment=신설.

4. **역할 자동통합 금지(§5.3·5.4·5.5·§40)**: Customer↔Affiliate·Referrer↔Referee·Seller↔Vendor↔Partner·동일 Organization 자동통합 금지(계약·계정·Provider Account·Legal Entity 확인). Affiliate/Creator/Seller=별도 Participant Identity·Account Binding. **Referrer/Referee 뒤바뀜=Critical Gap(§39)**.

5. **★이메일/전화 단독 확정 금지·Identity Confidence·Recipient Verification(§5.8·§27·§29·§41)**: 동일 이메일/전화만으로 Beneficiary/Payout Recipient 확정 금지 → Provider Account·External Participant ID·Reward Account·Contract·Tenant·Brand·Legal Entity·Verified Identifier·Payout Recipient·Historical Validity·Shared Identifier Risk 종합. Recipient Verification(Email/Phone/Account/Wallet/Bank Ownership·KYC/KYB·Tax Profile·Contract)·**만료/실패 지급경로 Block·Low-confidence/Multiple Match 자동 지급확정 금지**. 현행 crm identity=best-effort(confidence 미계산)·KYC/Verification 전무=GAP.

6. **금융 식별정보 원문 금지(§5.10·§22~24·§40)**: Bank Account·Wallet Credential·Tax Identifier·Beneficial Owner 상세 원문 저장 금지 → **Tokenized/Masked Reference만**(Owner Verification 상태만). 현행 금융 식별정보 저장 자체가 부재(신설 시 원칙 강제).

7. **Shared Account·Merge History·Historical Validity(§5.9·§25·§30·§31)**: Shared Reward Account=Owner/Co-owner/Contributor/Redeemer/Payout Recipient/Admin/Viewer 권한 기록·**전체를 개인 자동귀속 금지·Organization Reward를 개인 귀속 금지**. Multi-beneficiary=Allocation Method/Ratio/Amount/Currency/Validity. **Alias/Merge/Unmerge/Split/Transfer/Consolidation=Append-only·Merge 이후 과거 역할/Settlement 귀속 삭제·덮어쓰기 금지·Historical Validity(valid from/to) 보존**. 현행 crm merge=best-effort(원본 보존)이나 structured history 미보존=GAP·EPIC05 Merge 정합.

8. **정직·무후퇴·Coverage/Gap**: Identity Governance 부재=결함 아니라 **PROVIDER_LIMITATION/NOT_APPLICABLE 명시**(NO_DATA/오탐 금지). Candidate/Reconciliation/Coverage=전방호환. Critical Gap(잘못된 Beneficiary·Payout Recipient 무관·Destination Owner Verification 실패·Seller↔Legal Entity·Affiliate↔Commission·Creator↔Agency 불일치·Referrer/Referee 반전·Shared 개인귀속·Cross-Tenant/Cross-Legal-Entity·Merge 후 Settlement 귀속 변경·Tax/Beneficial Owner 누락·이메일 단독 확정·금융계좌 원문) 시 Access Review 차단. crm identity·referral_signup·PartnerPortal·AgencyPortal·`/api/agency/*`·`/api/partner/*` 보존(회귀 0). 기능후퇴 0.
