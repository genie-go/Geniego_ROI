## 2. Secure Delete/Anonymize/Pseudonymize/Aggregate (§86-89)
- **Secure Delete(§86)**: Database Row/Partition Drop·Object/Object Version Delete·Index Delete·Cache Evict·Queue Purge·**Cryptographic Erasure/Key Destruction**·Provider Delete API·File Shredding·Snapshot/Backup Expiry — 삭제완료 Evidence 기록.
- **Anonymization(§87)**: 직접식별자 제거·간접식별자 최소·**Rare Combination**·Free Text·Location·Timestamp Precision·Linkability·External ID·Model Feature·**Re-identification Risk**·Downstream Copies·Backup 검증.
- **Pseudonymization(§88)**: **≠삭제/익명화** — Key Separation·Access Control·Rotation·Re-link 권한·Purpose·Retention·Audit·Deletion Propagation.
- **Aggregation(§89)**: 최소 Group Size·**Small Cell Suppression**·Time/Geography Granularity·Outlier·Differential Privacy 필요성·Re-identification 검토·Source 삭제·Audit. (No-PII 집계 원칙·Decisioning 정합.)

## 3. Orphan/Shadow (§90-92) & Reconciliation (§93-95)
- **Orphan(§90)**: Owner 없는 Table·Consumer 없는 Dataset·Source 없는 Index·Manifest 없는 Archive·Policy 없는 Bucket·참조 없는 Export File·종료 Job Temporary File·**삭제 Tenant Data**·미등록 Provider Copy·오래된 Snapshot·Old Schema Shadow Table. **Shadow(§91)**: 개발자 임시복제 Table·분석 CSV·Debug Dump·**로컬 백업(dist.bak 278차)**·비공식 Object Storage·Staging DB Copy·Test 환경 운영데이터·AI Evaluation Copy·Notebook Export·Email Attachment·Shared Drive Export. **처리(§92)**: 발견→Scope→Owner→Data Category→Purpose→접근차단→Legal Hold 확인→Delete/Archive 결정→Evidence→Root Cause→**Recurrence Prevention**→Audit.
- **Reconciliation(§93)**: Registry Policy vs DB TTL/Object Lifecycle/Warehouse Partition/Cache TTL/Queue/Backup/Provider Retention · Expired Record vs 실제존재 · Legal Hold vs 삭제 · Deleted Subject vs Archive/Backup · Data Asset Registry vs 실제 Storage · Active Consumer vs Retained Data. **상태(§94)**: MATCH/POLICY_MISSING/IMPLEMENTATION_MISSING/**RETAINED_TOO_LONG/DELETED_TOO_EARLY**/ARCHIVE_MISMATCH/HOLD_MISMATCH/BACKUP_MISMATCH/PROVIDER_MISMATCH/ORPHAN_DATA/SHADOW_COPY/MANUAL_REVIEW/BLOCKED. **Drift(§95)**: TTL/Lifecycle Rule/Bucket Versioning/Backup 기간/Queue Retention/Index Snapshot/Provider 설정 변경·Policy Version 불일치·New Table/Cache/Export/AI Dataset 미등록.
