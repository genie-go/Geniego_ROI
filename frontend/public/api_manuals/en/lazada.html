Lazada
API Key Issuance Manual

A beginner-friendly, step-by-step guide to registering an app on the Lazada Open Platform to issue an App Key and App Secret, obtaining an access_token through shop authorization, and registering it in the GenieGo Integration Hub.

Est. timeAbout 5–15 min
Account neededLazada seller account
Keys issuedApp Key · App Secret
ResultAPI credentials

1. Before You Begin

Lazada API credentials are issued by registering an app on the Open Platform.

Required

Lazada seller account

To proceed with issuance, you need a Lazada seller account. Use this account to register as a developer on the Lazada Open Platform.

Keys issued

App Key · App Secret

When you create an app, an App Key and App Secret are issued. You then obtain an access_token through shop authorization.

2. Issuance Steps

Issue in the order below and register in GenieGo.

1

Register as a developer

Register as a developer on the Lazada Open Platform (open.lazada.com).

https://open.lazada.com
2

Create an app

When you create an app, an App Key and App Secret are issued.

3

Shop authorization

Complete shop authorization to obtain an access_token.

4

Verify the values

Verify the issued app_key and app_secret.

Once the App Key and App Secret are issued and you obtain an access_token through shop authorization, it has been issued correctly.

3. Information to Register in GenieGo

FieldDescription
app_keyThe issued App Key
app_secretThe issued App Secret (handle with care)
In the GenieGo Integration Hub [Register], enter your app_key and app_secret and save. Credentials are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM and masked on read.

4. Common Issues

IssueCauseSolution
Cannot create an appDeveloper registration on the Lazada Open Platform not completedRegister as a developer on the Lazada Open Platform (open.lazada.com) and then create an app.
Authentication failureShop authorization not completed, or access_token not issuedAfter issuing the App Key and App Secret, complete shop authorization to obtain an access_token.

Final Checklist

  • Completed developer registration on the Open Platform with a Lazada seller account.
  • Created an app and issued the App Key and App Secret.
  • Completed shop authorization and obtained an access_token.
  • Entered app_key and app_secret in GenieGo [Register] and saved.
