Amazon SP-API
API Issuance Manual

A step-by-step guide that anyone can follow to issue Amazon SP-API credentials (API key/token) and register them in the GenieGo Integration Hub.

Est. timeAbout 5–15 min

Steps7 steps
ResultAPI credentials

Issuance Steps

Follow the steps below to issue and register your credentials.

1

What you need: an Amazon Professional seller account. Individual accounts are not supported.

2

Sign in to Seller Central → go to the top menu [Apps & Services] > [Develop Apps].

3

Complete [Developer Profile registration], submitting your organization info and your data-use purpose and security details (approval required).

4

In the Developer Console, click [Add new app client] to create an app, which issues LWA credentials (client_id/client_secret).

5

Obtain a refresh token via self-authorize.

6

Find the Marketplace ID and Seller ID you are selling on.

7

In GenieGo [Register], enter your refresh_token, client_id, client_secret, marketplace_id, and seller_id, then save.

Final Checklist

  • What you need: an Amazon Professional seller account. Individual accounts are not supported.
  • Sign in to Seller Central → go to the top menu [Apps & Services] > [Develop Apps].
  • Complete [Developer Profile registration], submitting your organization info and your data-use purpose and security details (approval required).
  • In the Developer Console, click [Add new app client] to create an app, which issues LWA credentials (client_id/client_secret).
  • Obtain a refresh token via self-authorize.
  • Find the Marketplace ID and Seller ID you are selling on.
  • In GenieGo [Register], enter your refresh_token, client_id, client_secret, marketplace_id, and seller_id, then save.
Once entered in the GenieGo Integration Hub [Register], your keys are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM and masked on read.
Keep your keys/secrets safe and never expose them publicly. If you suspect a leak, rotate (re-issue) immediately.
