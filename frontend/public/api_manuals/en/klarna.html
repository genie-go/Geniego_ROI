Issuance Steps

Follow the steps below to issue and register your credentials.

1

Requirements: an approved Klarna merchant contract (BNPL).

2

Sign in to the Merchant Portal at portal.klarna.com.

3

Under [Settings] > [Klarna API credentials], create your API username/password and note your region (eu/na/oc).

4

In GenieGo [Register], enter username, password, and region, then save.

Final Checklist

  • Requirements: an approved Klarna merchant contract (BNPL).
  • Sign in to the Merchant Portal at portal.klarna.com.
  • Under [Settings] > [Klarna API credentials], create your API username/password and note your region (eu/na/oc).
  • In GenieGo [Register], enter username, password, and region, then save.
Once entered in the GenieGo Integration Hub [Register], your keys are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM and masked on read.
Keep your keys/secrets safe and never expose them publicly. If you suspect a leak, rotate (re-issue) immediately.
