Naver Pay
API Key Issuance Manual

Naver Pay is a channel where credentials are handed to you by a representative after your merchant sign-up (contract) is completed. This manual walks through registering the Client ID, Client Secret, and Chain ID you receive in the GenieGo Integration Hub.

Est. timeVaries with contract and review period

ResultPayment integration credentials

1. Before You Begin

With Naver Pay, you don't self-issue keys; credentials are delivered to you after the merchant contract is completed.

Naver Pay is not a method where you issue keys directly in a console. Once your merchant sign-up (contract) is completed, a representative hands the credentials to you.
Important: This is not ordinary self-issuance. After the merchant sign-up (contract) is completed, the Partner ID and Client ID/Secret are delivered to you.

2. Issuance Application Process

Proceed with the contract in the order below and register the credentials you receive.

1

Apply for the merchant contract

Apply for and complete the merchant contract at the Naver Pay Business Center.

2

Complete review and contract

Continue until the review and contract are complete. The duration may vary depending on the review schedule.

3

Receive credentials

After the review and contract are complete, a representative gives you the Client ID, Client Secret, and Chain ID.

Once you have received the Client ID, Client Secret, and Chain ID from the representative, you are ready to register.

3. Information to Register in GenieGo

FieldDescription
client_idThe Client ID you received
client_secretThe Client Secret you received (handle with care)
chain_idThe Chain ID you received
Enter these in the GenieGo Integration Hub [Register] and save; the credentials are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM and masked on read.
