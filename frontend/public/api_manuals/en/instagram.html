Corporate Instagram Business Account Conversion Manual

A beginner-friendly guide that helps companies, brands, online stores, and live-commerce operators convert their Instagram account to a business/creator account and prepare for Meta Business Suite and API integration.

Recommended accountBusiness account for companies

1. Business Account vs. Creator Account

For corporate use, a business account is usually the most suitable choice.

Business Account

Business account

Suitable for companies, brands, online stores, logistics services, live commerce, and online sales. Advantageous for ads, insights, Meta Business Suite, and API integration.

Creator Account

Creator account

Suitable for influencers, content creators, and personal brand operators. For enterprise API, shopping, and ad operations, a business account is more stable.

Recommendation: Convert your corporate Instagram account to a business account.

2. What to Prepare Before Creating an Account

  • Corporate email address
  • Brand name or company name
  • Candidate Instagram username
  • Company logo or profile image
  • Company introduction text
  • Website URL
  • An existing or to-be-created Facebook page

3. Create an Instagram Account

1

Install the Instagram app

Install and launch the Instagram app on your smartphone.

App Store / Google Play → Instagram
2

Sign up

Create a new account with a corporate email or phone number.

Create new account
3

Set a username

Enter a username that matches your brand name or is easy to search.

e.g., geniego_global / ocl_korea
4

Enter basic profile information

Enter your logo, introduction text, and website URL.

Edit profile

4. Switch to a Professional Account

1

Open the profile menu

Tap the profile icon at the bottom of the Instagram app.

Profile
2

Go to Settings and privacy

Tap the ☰ menu in the top right, then go to Settings and privacy.

☰ → Settings and privacy
3

Select Account type and tools

In the account management menu, find the option to switch to a professional account.

Account type and tools
4

Switch to a professional account

Skip past the intro screens and choose to switch to a professional account.

Switch to professional account

5. Choose a Business Account

1

Select account type

Choose Business from Creator and Business.

Select Business
2

Select a category

Pick a category that matches your company's nature.

Select category
3

Enter contact information

Enter information customers can check, such as email, phone number, and address.

Business contact info
4

Confirm conversion is complete

When the Professional Dashboard appears on your profile, the conversion is complete.

Professional Dashboard

7. Connect a Facebook Page

1

Go to the Professional Dashboard

Open the Professional Dashboard from your Instagram profile.

Profile → Professional Dashboard
2

Connect a Facebook page

Select an existing Facebook page or create a new one.

Connect Facebook page
3

Check admin permissions

Make sure you have admin permission for the Facebook page you want to connect.

Page Admin permission
Connecting a Facebook page makes it easier to run ads, check insights, and manage Meta Business Suite.

8. Connect Meta Business Suite

1

Access Meta Business Suite

Open Meta Business Suite in your browser.

business.facebook.com
2

Create a business account

Enter your company name, name, and email to create a business account.

Create business
3

Add the Instagram account

Add your Instagram account in Business settings.

Business settings → Accounts → Instagram accounts
4

Verify the Facebook page connection

Confirm that your Instagram account is connected to the correct Facebook page.

Check connected assets

9. Business Verification

1

Open Business settings

Go to Business settings in Meta Business Suite.

Business settings
2

Go to the Security Center

Select the Security Center from the left menu.

Security Center
3

Start business verification

Enter your business registration certificate, company name, address, website, and so on.

Business verification
4

Check the review result

After Meta completes its review, check the verification status.

Verification Status

  • Created a corporate Instagram account.
